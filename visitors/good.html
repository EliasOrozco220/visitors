The End

You killed your last friend and decide to go in the spaceship. Everything from there goes as planned, the ship works and you're off. Once you leave the planet, you then look for civilization in another planet with the spaceship's automatic navigator. You don't know if anyone else has made it and awaiting for other's arrival. With the lack of research on these creatures, you may not know wether it's contagious or not. This is the end of your life on Earth and maybe the start to a new one else where. But only time can tell, even if time itself is non-existant now.
